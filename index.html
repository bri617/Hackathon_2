About

Lee came to the United States in 2017 from outside of Seoul in South Korea. She had a dream to sell her Korean fried chicken and introduce our community to hers.“Sure, I really wanted to spread Korean culture in the Valley. I am so proud of myself and my team, even the Valley that everyone enjoys our food,” Lee told us. She adds her restaurant also showcases other sides of Korean culture. “I enjoy the K-pop music,” Lee said. “Ever since I was born in 1990, I was a big fan of K-pop. I love Korean movies!”
